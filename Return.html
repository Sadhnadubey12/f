RETURNS/EXCHANGE AT UNIQUE STYLE STORES

Most items are returnable in our stores. To check which items cannot be returned in our stores, please call our customer care on 1-800-419-6648. You may visit any Shoppers' Stop store in India and return the merchandise in original condition (unused, unwashed, unaltered, untampered, and in saleable condition) with original tags, invoice, and packaging intact.

If you return any merchandise purchased on Shoppersstop.com at any Shoppers Stop store, you shall be issued a Shoppers' Stop credit note against receipt of the merchandise, which may be redeemed against any purchases of an equivalent amount from any Shoppers' Stop store within 180 days.

The issue of the Shoppers Stop credit note, to the person returning the merchandise, against the returned merchandise shall be sufficient discharge to Shoppers Stop for the return of the merchandise, regardless of whether the payment for the original purchase was made by.

Please note that the original Shoppers Stop credit note must be produced while redeeming against any purchases. We shall not be responsible in the event the original credit note is misplaced or destroyed.

If you request a return or exchange through Shoppers Stop courier collection for any reason within the specified days as mentioned above or as per the timeline mentioned on the product details page, we request you to return the product in original condition (unused, unwashed, unaltered, untampered & in saleable condition) with original tags, invoice & original packaging intact, failing which return/exchange may not be possible.

You may write to us customercare@uniquestyle.com and request a return or exchange. We will arrange for the merchandise to be collected from the delivery address through courier within 2 working days from the date of request raised.
